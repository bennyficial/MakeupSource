Industry Influencers

These are some of the major influencers in the make-up industry today

Patrick Starr

Twenty-seven-year-old Patrick Starrr – born Patrick Simondac – turned his love of makeup into a YouTube empire complete with international business trips and 1.8 million subscribers.

Manny Gutierrez

Manny Gutierrez, at just age 25, he’s already a major social media star, who repeatedly posts photos of himself with full, vivid makeup on Instagram and YouTube. He’s part of a growing trend of men wearing makeup and altering gender stereotypes, and his career online has exploded in just a few short years pony's. He has more than 4 million followers and 2.8 million subscribers on Instagram and Youtube, respectively.

Kandee Johnson

She won a Streamy Award in the Beauty category in 2016. She was a guest judge on How Do I Look and has been featured in Elle, Glamour, AOL, Yahoo, and a video series with Glamour magazine. She graduated from beauty school but realized she hated doing hair. She solely wanted to focus on make-up and soon enough landed her first make-up artist job on VH1's I Love the 80's

Pony

With over 600,000 subscribers on YouTube, Pony is one of South Korea's most famous beauty gurus. Her real name is Hye-Min Park, though nearly everyone refers to her by her equine nickname. She's been blogging about Korean beauty for years, so if you're looking for K-beauty inspiration, look no further than Pony.
